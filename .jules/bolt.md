## 2025-05-23 - [ChefMini and AddMeal Stabilization]
**Learning:** `ChefMini`, `AddMeal`, and `RateLimitProgressBar` were re-rendering unnecessarily on every parent `App` render (e.g. typing in inputs, toggling modals). This was because they were not memoized and, crucially, the `onSave`, `onLogMeal`, and `onDelete` handlers passed to them were being recreated on every render.
**Action:** Wrapped `ChefMini`, `AddMeal`, and `RateLimitProgressBar` in `React.memo`. Wrapped `handleSaveRecipe`, `handleDeleteRecipe`, `handleLogRecipe`, and `handleSavePlan` in `useCallback` in `App.tsx` to provide stable references. Also reordered handler definitions in `App.tsx` to avoid "use before define" errors in `useCallback` dependencies.
